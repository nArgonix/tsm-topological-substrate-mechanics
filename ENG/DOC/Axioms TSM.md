## 1. Formal Axiomatic System of the TSM Theory (Based on Chapter 0)

### Axiom I: Non-Emptiness and Discreteness of the Substrate (0-Matrix)

Physical space is not a non-physical void, but a dense substrate (0-Matrix) filled with fundamental, indivisible, structural elements of matter at the sub-Planckian scale, termed 0-particles.

* 0-particles are characterized by an identical inertial mass (serving exclusively as a parameter of resistance to changes in the state of oscillation, rather than a source of gravity).
* Each 0-particle possesses a hard core and a strictly defined, non-zero intrinsic volume $V_0$:

$$\tag{0.1.01} V_0 > 0$$

### Axiom II: Absolute Sub-Planckian Kinetics and the Emergence of Time

At the deepest, sub-Planckian level, 4D mechanics is absolute (non-relativistic), which precludes the emergence of relativistic infinities.

* Primordial 0-particles undergo continuous micro-oscillations and collisions at tremendous velocities $v$, significantly exceeding the macroscopic wave velocity $c$:

$$\tag{0.1.02} v \gg c$$

* Primordial micro-oscillations do not measure or define the passage of time; they serve solely as the source of background kinetic energy, global pressure, and stresses. Relativistic time and spacetime are fully emergent phenomena, arising at higher levels as a result of periodic processes of organized matter.

### Axiom III: Kinetic Origin of Elasticity and the Packing Limit

The theory categorically rejects the existence of primordial force fields or attractive interactions between 0-particles.

* The entirety of the substrate's elasticity and stress gradients arises exclusively from spatial collisional dynamics and momentum transfer within the bounded volume of lattice cages (Wigner-Seitz cells).
* Mechanical compression shortens the mean free path of 0-particles, triggering an avalanche-like increase in collision frequency and thrust. This process possesses an insurmountable geometric limit—the maximum sphere packing fraction $\phi_{\text{max}}$, at which the cores of the 0-particles come into physical contact at their boundaries:

$$\tag{0.2.01} \phi \le \phi_{\text{max}}$$

### Axiom IV: Finiteness and Global Jamming of Fundamental Space

**Axiom IV: Finiteness and Global Jamming of Fundamental Space** Fundamental space is a 4-dimensional, finite, geometrically closed manifold (the equivalent of a hypersphere), devoid of physical boundaries. The substrate is entirely filled with 0-particles and, from its initial state, exists in a state of global jamming. The global geometric packing fraction $\phi$ exceeds the critical value $\phi_c$:

$$\tag{0.3.01} \phi \ge \phi_c$$

In the Zero State, 0-particles are permanently confined within lattice cells, thereby lacking translational freedom (macroscopic flow). The substrate constitutes a rigid, elastic topological glass in 4D, permanently preserving the baseline configurational stress.

**Critical Displacement Clause (Boundary Plasticity Exception):** In the low- and intermediate-energy regimes, the absence of translational freedom for 0-particles is absolute, ensuring the structural stability of the substrate and the topological nodes trapped within it. However, an extreme physical case is permitted: when the local shear stress tensor or pressure gradient exceeds a critical threshold for plastic activation $\sigma_{\text{crit}}$ (the Big Event regime or the cores of macroscopic plasmoids), the substrate locally breaks through the jamming barrier. This leads to a forced, plastic flow of the Substrate, wherein 0-particles gain temporary translational freedom (critical displacement), enabling them to change their parent cells and undergo topological reconfiguration of the network. Following energy dissipation, the system immediately returns to a state of deep configurational freezing ($\phi \ge \phi_{\text{lock}}$), trapping the newly formed defects.

### Axiom V: Self-Regulation and Finitistic Relaxation of the Substrate

Every point of the 0-Matrix tends toward a state of mechanical relaxation, wherein the collisional thrust forces from all sides perfectly balance each other.

* In the low-energy regime, a local excess of stress cannot remain permanent; it is instantaneously and completely dissipated via waves into neighboring regions through a cascade of contact collisions, unifying the pressure within the system. This process occurs not only at the micro-scale but also features a global component: the entire 0-Matrix undergoes a slow, evolutionary, large-scale relaxation following its primordial torsion, which determines long-term changes in the wave propagation parameters within the Substrate (redshift effect; a detailed description of the mechanism and galactic structures can be found in a separate cosmological chapter).

### Axiom VI: Emergence of the 3-Brane and Quantized Matter (The Big Event)

As a result of a primordial, external disturbance of extreme energy (the Big Event), a plastic, permanent deformation (topological torsion) occurred within the rigid 0-Matrix.

* A permanent topological defect was formed in the shape of a three-dimensional hypersurface—a 3-brane, with dimension $D-1$:

$$\tag{0.5.01} D-1 = 3$$

* The brane inherits the elastic properties of the substrate and confines matter within its interior. Extreme pressure mega-fluctuations accompanying this event locally exceeded the plasticity threshold $\phi_{\text{lock}}$, leading to a geometric reconfiguration of the cells and the looping of momentum vectors, thereby generating stable, quantized, 4-dimensional topological nodes with a non-zero winding number $\mathcal{W}$ (the first fermions):

$$\tag{0.5.02} \mathcal{W} \neq 0$$

### Axiom VII: Physical Finiteness and the Ontology of Plasmoids

In the real world, infinities do not exist. Mathematical concepts in which density, pressure, temperature, or curvature tend toward infinity ($\infty$) are solely artifacts of imperfect continuous models:

$$\tag{0.6.01} \lim X \neq \infty$$

* Due to constraints arising from $V_0 > 0$ and $\phi_{\text{max}}$, gravitational collapse to a dimensionless point is categorically forbidden. Gravitational singularities and classical "black holes" do not exist.
* Astronomical objects at the centers of galaxies are degenerate, macroscopic entanglements of the substrate with a finite, extreme density, defined as plasmoids.

### Updated Principle: Kinetics of 0-Particles and "Topological Pressure"

* **Identity of baseline parameters:** Every 0-particle in the universe possesses exactly the same fundamental and invariant rest mass ($m_0$) and an identical, discrete momentum ($p_0$) of a single "impact".
* **Sphere of oscillation as a spatial variable:** The sole parameter differentiating the states of 0-particles is their velocity (frequency) of oscillation ($v_{\text{osc}}$) within their assigned sphere of oscillation (local spatial cell).
* **Principle of distortion and pressure:** When local space undergoes compression under the influence of a 4-dimensional topological bending (a node), the volume of the 0-particle's sphere of oscillation decreases. In accordance with the conservation of system parameters, the 0-particle must move faster within a smaller space.
* **Generation of interaction force:** A drastic increase in oscillation velocity implies a **multiplication of the number of collisions** (impacts) of a given 0-particle against neighboring elements of the Matrix per unit of time. Although the momentum of a single impact ($p_0$) remains constant, the increased frequency of impacts generates higher local mechanical pressure. This kinetic stress determines the force with which the distorted region interacts with its immediate surroundings.
